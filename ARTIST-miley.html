Miley Ray Cyrus is an American singer, songwriter, and actress.She has been recognized for her musical versatility and continual artistic reinventions. Cyrus has been referred to as the "Teen Queen" of the 2000s pop culture and regarded as one of the few examples of a child star who went on to have a successful career as an adult. She was ranked as the ninth-greatest Billboard 200 female artist of all time, and has been featured in Billboard's "Greatest of All Time Artists" in 2019. She emerged as a teen idol while portraying the titular character of the Disney Channel television series Hannah Montana (2006-2011).
